About Me

I got into programming while I was working as a tech analyst at UNC. I wanted to try something new and challenge myself so I chose a couple coding courses in the Computer Science department. The thrill of coding was totally new, and the payoff was unlike any feeling I'd experienced before. I decided it was time to pursue this new found passion, which brought me to the UNC Coding Boot Camp, which I recently completed.

I love learning new technologies, and I believe that I thrive on it. My current job as a tech analyst forces me to keep up with the changing and varying needs of faculty and staff in the Arts and Sciences college. The ability to grow with my departments and learn to support new technologies translated very nicely to my study of programming, where we were learning new languages and tools every week.
